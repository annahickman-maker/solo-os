## POV

The fastest path to first sales is a focused 90-day sprint with a real launch window - not forever-consistent posting and hoping something happens.

## The Idea Behind It

The mainstream advice is 'just stay consistent for 1-2 years.' Anna says this is the slow road that burns creative brains out. A concentrated launch sprint with a defined window matches how creative freelancers actually work.

## Why I Believe This

Anna's first launch: 300 people on a waitlist, 3 emails, $1,500 in 24 hours. The marble jar model - build trust in a sprint, then spend it in a focused launch window. Most sales happen at the start and end. Longer launches just create a lull in the middle.
