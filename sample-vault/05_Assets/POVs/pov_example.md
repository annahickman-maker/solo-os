# Example POV - the headline of your point of view

POVs (points of view) are your distinct, non-borrowed perspectives on your topic. Each POV lives as a separate markdown file in `05_Assets/POVs/`.

The dashboard mines POVs as raw material for content, sales pages, and audience copy. The more you write, the more the AI features have to work with.

This is an example. Replace it with your own POVs, one per file.
