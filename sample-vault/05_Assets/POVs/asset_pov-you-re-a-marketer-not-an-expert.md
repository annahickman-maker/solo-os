## POV

You don't have to be the best at what you do. You have to be really good at getting people to pay you for what you do. The most knowledgeable person is rarely the most successful.

## The Idea Behind It

The creative freelancer world worships craft mastery. Get better at design, get better at writing, get better at your skill - and the clients will come. Anna flips it: marketing ability is the actual bottleneck, not expertise.

## Why I Believe This

Creative freelancers with smaller portfolios but stronger positioning and content consistently out-earn more talented peers who rely on word of mouth. The skill gap between mid and top is tiny. The marketing gap is enormous.
