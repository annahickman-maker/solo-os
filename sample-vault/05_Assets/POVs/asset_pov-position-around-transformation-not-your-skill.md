## POV

Not 'I'm a web designer' but 'I help people get more consistent sales so they can quit their day job.' Position around the result your person wants, not the service you deliver.

## The Idea Behind It

Every freelancer positions around what they do - their craft, their niche, their aesthetic. Anna says this makes you interchangeable. The transformation ladders up to health, wealth, or relationships. The service is just the vehicle.

## Why I Believe This

People don't pay $27K because someone has the perfect headline. They pay because they trust the person and believe in the result. You could take the exact same offer and not get the same results because you don't have the brand they've built. Trust is the moat, not the niche statement.
