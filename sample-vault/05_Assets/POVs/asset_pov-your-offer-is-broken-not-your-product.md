## POV

Nine times out of ten, if something isn't working in your business, it's your offer that needs to change - not your product. Stop adding more deliverables and start selling the result.

## The Idea Behind It

Most creators respond to low sales by adding more modules, more templates, more lessons - assuming more equals more valuable. Anna separates the product (what you sell) from the offer (how you sell it) and says the offer is almost always the broken part.

## Why I Believe This

Her $24 Content Workflow example: the product is a Notion template. The offer is 'a proven content system to attract your first web design client in 30 days.' Same product, completely different value. People don't buy more stuff - they buy a more believable result.
