## POV

Short-form grows your audience. Long-form is what actually converts. A buyer spends seven hours with your content before purchasing - you can't build that in 30-second clips.

## The Idea Behind It

The prevailing advice is to post daily short-form content everywhere. Anna argues this builds an audience of consumers, not buyers, and the moment you stop posting the traffic dies. Long-form compounds.

## Why I Believe This

Anna's own proof: $20K/month from 5 evergreen YouTube videos. One 15-min video every other week for a year builds 6.5 hours of trust. Short-form has a short lifespan and lower conversions. Long-form keeps selling for years.
