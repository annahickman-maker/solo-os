## POV

Give the knowledge away free. Sell the implementation. If people feel like you're gatekeeping the best stuff for your paid products, they'll sense it and it will destroy any trust you could have built.

## The Idea Behind It

Most course creators and coaches hold back their best frameworks for paid tiers. Anna says this kills trust - the exact thing that makes people buy. Generosity is the sales strategy.

## Why I Believe This

Information is already commoditized by AI. Anyone can Google steps. The value is in tools, templates, coaching, and access - not in knowing the theory. Full-value free content builds the reputation that commands premium prices.
