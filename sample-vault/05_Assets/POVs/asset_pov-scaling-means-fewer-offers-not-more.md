## POV

Diversification is a distraction. Scale by making each customer worth more with the same offer at increasing levels of proximity - not by building more products for more audiences.

## The Idea Behind It

The standard scaling advice is to add more offers, more funnels, more traffic sources. Anna says the real lever is lifetime value per customer: the same person, the same result, with increasing support and price.

## Why I Believe This

Her product suite model - digital product to group coaching to 1:1 - serves one buyer on one journey. Building three products for three audiences scatters your marketing so nothing converts. One front door, then upsell from inside.
