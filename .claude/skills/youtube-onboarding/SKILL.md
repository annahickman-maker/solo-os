# YouTube setup (retired)

There's no separate YouTube onboarding anymore. Everything the YouTube skills
need comes from two places you already have:

1. **Your Solo OS foundation.** Run /solopreneur-onboarding if you haven't - it
   sets your positioning, audience, story, core IP, offer suite, and voice.

2. **Your Content page.** Click your avatar to choose who your videos are for
   (your content focus avatar), and fill the "what you point viewers to" box
   with your YouTube CTA.

That's it. Every YouTube skill reads those automatically.
